Basics of watercolour for the fledgling artist – In person lesson

In the studio, every Thursday between 6.30pm-8.30pm (from September 16th – October 21st, 2021)

Tap into your inner artist with this informal art class. Part art class, part social and 100% fun, in this series of classes I will take you through the basics of watercolour and other arty related things. This one’s for all the beginners, the “I-have-no-idea-where-to start-ers”, as well as for anyone who wants to improve their skills, meet new people and get chatting away to other creatives.

This is your chance to get creative, try something new, brush upon those, errmm, brush skills and socialise…what a lovely way to spend a Thursday evening hey!So, if you’re a resident in Brecon or the surrounding areas or simply visiting the beautiful Brecon Beacons, then come and join me at Laura Dennis studio & Gallery for a taste of something different.

Life drawing Sunday sessions – In person lessonS

In the studio, every Sunday between 12.00pm-2.30pm (from September 19th – October 24th, 2021)

Join us for a Sunday Social at this Life Drawing Session. This one is all about providing you with the opportunity to draw from life in a relaxed and informal environment. Everyone is welcome, from total beginners to those well versed in artistic pursuits, this class is designed to give you access to a life model and explore your creativity with the human form.

BYO materials, tea and coffee (and biscuits!) provided, plus some very chilled playlists to keep the ambiance nice and relaxed.
