Makers Corner


In Makers corner I hope to showcase some of the great work done by my fellow Welsh artists and artisans, these people possess wonderful and unique skills in their chosen craft that deserves to shared and celebrated, all of which are available to buy in my lovely gallery.

Photographs by Paul Williams


Paul is a Brecon-based photographer, adventurer & wildlife expert. He captures our beautiful National Park at its best, relying on a in-depth knowledge of hiking routes to create striking photographs of Brecon's local surroundings.

Meta earth paints - Wanderlust edition by Jackie Yeomans


These beautiful paints are hand-made in small batches from foraged soils. The refined pigments are bound with gum arabic, distilled water and glycerine creating a totally organic single pigment paint. Made as a watercolour, yet with thicker, opaque application can work as a gouche.

Wooden spoons by Grant Withington


Grant uses tarditional techniques to handcraft beechwood into durable & beautiful pieces for your kitchen. Each spoon is hand made & finished with a slyod knife (no sanding involed), leaving a delicately faceted surface unique to each piece. Made in Mid-Wales.
